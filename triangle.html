Triangle definition

A triangle is a shape that is made up of three lines and three angles.

Equilateral triangle

An equilateral triangle has all three sides equal in length. Its three angles are also equal and they are each 60Â°. An equilateral triangle is also an equiangular triangle since all its angles are equal.

Isosceles triangle

An isosceles triangle has two sides of equal length. The angles opposite the equal sides are also equal.

Scalene triangle

A scalene triangle has no sides of equal length. Its angles are also all different in size.
